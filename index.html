About

Alex Argilés Acevedo is an Emmy Award-winning Producer driven by a passion for crafting compelling stories that resonate on a human level.

Born in Havana and based in New York City, Alex blends cultural authenticity with creative storytelling to create impactful content — his stories often deal with the sacrifices we make for our loved ones.

Starting his career at Florida State University's prestigious Film program, Alex quickly became known for his creative vision and sharp planning, from budgeting to scheduling. His journey has led to collaborations with leading artists, networks, and production companies, earning multiple accolades, including five NYC Emmy nominations.

Whether producing groundbreaking documentaries or dynamic television specials, Alex's work exemplifies excellence and heart, continuously pushing boundaries in the world of media and entertainment.
